# About

I am an AI builder and entrepreneur. I am currently the co-founder and CTO of
[Galtea AI][galtea], and Visiting Researcher at [Barcelona Supercomputing Center (BSC-CNS)][bsc],

Galtea platform helps developers develop AI Agents generative AI safely and extreme speed, through end-to-end evaluation powered by synthetic test case generation and and use-case specific metrics. 

Previously, I was Engineering Manager and Team Leader of Speech at the Language Technologies
Unit of BSC-CNS, and before that, I co-founded the workers collective [Col·lectivaT][coop].

Here you can find further information about my current and past
[scientific work][science] and my appearances in the [media][in-media] in the
article or interview format.

I used to do [podcasts](/podcast) (in Turkish) about history of science and social movements.
